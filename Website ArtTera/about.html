why choose us?

ArtTera is a website that relies on artists. Artists play a role as providers of painting works of art that will be sold to customers or collectors of works of art. On this website, artists don't only sell paintings on canvas, but can also sell works using items around them, for example, such as painting using shoes, jackets, and much more.
